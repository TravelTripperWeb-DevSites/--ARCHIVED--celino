TIMELESS LUXURY REIMAGINED

Culturally Influenced, Unspoken Elegance

Set to debut summer 2017 on iconic Ocean Drive, The Celino will bring a new lifestyle of indulgence to South Beach, a personality rich in fashion fused with Latin charisma and tropical class, a vibrant reinterpretation of a party that happened on New Year’s Eve 1940. By bridging the historic Park Central Hotel with a fully modern, loft-style, ocean front oasis, a new standard of luxury will be set on Ocean Drive. The Celino will encompass the ambiance of timeless allure and transform itself into a memorable destination for entertainment and noteworthy moments. Get your dancing shoes ready!

Our Style

The Celino embodies the charm of a fashion Maison, its name will be a symbol of a new signature style – Floridita, a fusion of 1940s Miami Beach glamour with undertones of simple, classic Floridian accents. This unique style will bring back memories of awe-inspiring guests in sequined birds of paradise dresses, neck ties, sheer blouses and vintage prints all with a 2017 slant.
