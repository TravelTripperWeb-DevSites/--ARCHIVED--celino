TIMELESS LUXURY REIMAGINED

The Celino brings a new lifestyle of indulgence to South Beach, a personality rich in fashion fused with Latin charisma and tropical class, a vibrant reinterpretation of a party that happened on New Year’s Eve 1940. By bridging the historic Park Central Hotel with a fully modern, loft-style, ocean front oasis, a new standard of luxury will be set on Ocean Drive. The Celino will encompass the ambiance of timeless allure and transform itself into a memorable destination for entertainment and noteworthy moments. Get your dancing shoes ready!
