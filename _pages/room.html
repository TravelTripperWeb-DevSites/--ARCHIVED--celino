CLASSIC KING ROOM

The Classic King features one King bed and offers tropical elegance in a fresh and inviting, all-natural setting. Pastel salmon accents provide a sense of Latin flavor and warmth to the interior. The signature finishes include white washed oak hardwood floors and headboard, a 100% hand tufted New Zealand wool area rug and locally produced graphic artwork. The wash room features contemporary fixtures, a floating pedestal sink and a luxe walk-in shower.
