MEET CELINO

The Celino Hotel pays homage to Ocean Drive circa 1940s, transporting guests to a new take on a bygone era of glitz and glamour. Its personality is rich in fashion and fused with Latin charisma and tropical class, offering a new lifestyle of indulgence to South Beach.

The Celino Hotel embodies the charm of a fashion Maison, its name will be a symbol of a new signature style – Floridita, Miami Beach glamour paired with undertones of simple, classic Floridian accents. This unique style will bring back memories of awe-inspiring guests in sequined birds of paradise dresses, neck ties, sheer blouses and vintage prints all with a 2017 slant. Always full of excitement balanced with unspoken elegance and cultural influences, The Celino Hotel will lead an Ocean Drive revival.

LOCATION

Located beachfront in the heart of South Beach on the southern end of iconic Ocean Drive next to the SoFi neighborhood, steps from the Atlantic Ocean and with direct access to downtown Miami and the Port of Miami via MacArthur Causeway. The Celino is less than seven miles from Miami’s top urban destinations including Wynwood, Brickell, Midtown and just blocks from South Pointe Park, Lincoln Road and Española Way.
